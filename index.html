How to use this tool:

Basically the idea is to copy each ranking from browser and paste it into field next to "checkpoint" button.

Procedure is as follows:
1) Go to Battleknight ranking, copy whole text (select all with ctrl+a, copy selection with ctrl+c),
2) Go to Bkble Online, click field next to button "checkpoint" and hit paste (ctrl+v). In bottom table there will be shown list of knights with increase for now equal 0,
3) After few minutes refresh ranking in Battleknight and do point 1 and 2 again. If there were loot change it will be detected. It means there were loot change between first and second ranking check. Try to attack knights with biggest loot increase. You will have bigger chance that knight will still have something on account :)

Checkpoint button can be used whenever you want. It will save new baseline. Increases in loot will dropped. To make it clear let assume you are using Bkble Online for 1 hour and there are lots of changes. It is not clear not which loot has changed lately. Hit "checkpoint" button and comparison will be done from last ranking check.

There is no connection between Bkble Online and Battleknight. It do not violate game rules. Basically using Bkble Online is same as using pen and paper or Excel to count differences. It is more quick thought and more simple I hope ;) Feel free to use it as much as you want.

To reset state just reload this page.

Tested on Firefox and Chrome. Other browser should work also without problems.


Procedura użycia w "pigułce":
1) Wejść na stronę rankingu, nacisnąć ctrl+a (lub z menu "zaznacz wszystko") i skopiować tekst do schowka naciskając ctrl+c (lub z menu "kopiuj"). Uwaga: w przeciwieństwie do "starego" Bkble nie trzeba kopiować i wklejać źródła strony, wystarczy tekst na stronie rankingu.
2) Przejść na stronę Bkble Online i w pole tekstowe na górze po prawej wkleić informacje ze schowka używając ctrl+v (lub "wklej" z menu).
3) Powtórzyć 1) i 2) po odczekaniu jakiegoś czasu (np. 5 minut) uprzednio odświeżając stronę rankingu.
4) Powtórzyć 1), 2) i 3) z iloma rankingami się chce i ile razy się chce ;)

Tajemniczy przycisk "Checkpoint" służy do zresetowania przyrostów, przycisk "x" przy każdej linii służy do ignorowania rycerza.

Jeżeli chcecie zresetować całkowicie Bkble Online wejdźcie na stronę ponownie uprzednio ją zamykając bądź kliknijcie w pole adresu przeglądarki i naciśnijcie enter.

W razie dalszych niejasności możecie napisać do mnie - postaram się pomóc. Mail znajduje się w ostatniej zakładce.

Testowane na Chrome i Firefox. Pod pozostałymi przeglądarkami tez powinno działać.

This tool is online version of Bkble - standalone Java program for loot increases monitoring.
Bkble was created by me in first decade of XX century when I was studying. Back than I was also very involved Battleknight player. It was published and shared for free with all interested players. Years after that I've made online version (page you are looking at) to extend reach of this free tool.

See something not working or have an idea how to improve Bkble? You can contact me using pisklak83@gmail.com.

Have fun! :)
